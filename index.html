About Me

My name is Duygu Keklik. I was born on July 11, 2000 in Ankara, Turkey.

I completed my primary education at Ahmet Haşim Primary School. I graduated from Tapu ve Kadastro Vocational and Technical Anatolian High School. Between 2018–2020, I obtained an associate degree from Hacettepe University — Mapping and Cadastre Department. Later, I transferred to the bachelor program through the DGS (Vertical Transfer Exam) and I am currently a 4th-year Geomatics Engineering student at Hacettepe University.

Internships

  • General Directorate of Land Registry and Cadastre (TKGM)
  • Etimesgut-Sincan Cadastre Directorate
  • Buğra Proje Engineering Company

Profile

I am passionate about GNSS, surveying and geospatial technologies. I enjoy working on projects that combine field surveying and software-based geospatial analysis. I aim to contribute to innovative geospatial projects and research.
